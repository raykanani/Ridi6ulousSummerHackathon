Ridi6ulous Summer Hackathon

August 20th 2016 [10am - 6pm]

Mark the end of summer with an exclamation mark by building the most ridiculous hack you can think of. Use code, cardboard, food, playdough and any other wacky materials you can think of.

Bring your kids, grandparents, sister in-law and anyone else you want to spend a day with building cool, stupid and weird stuff with.
